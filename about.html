Our Story

Our Story

How Courage Charity began

From Vision to Reality

Courage Charity Organization was founded in 2015 by Brian Busiku, a social worker who grew up in Kampala and witnessed firsthand the plight of street children. What began as a small outreach program with just five volunteers has grown into a registered nonprofit organization that has helped over 500 children.

Our journey started with providing basic meals and temporary shelter to children living on the streets of Kampala. As we understood their needs better, we expanded our programs to include education, healthcare, family reunification, and vocational training.

Today, we operate a dedicated shelter home, run educational programs in partnership with local schools, and provide comprehensive support to help street children rebuild their lives and reintegrate into society.

Why We Exist

In Kampala alone, there are an estimated 15,000 children living on the streets. These children face daily challenges including hunger, violence, exploitation, and lack of access to education and healthcare. Courage Charity exists to address these issues and give these children hope for a better future.

Our Founder

Meet Brian Busiku

Brian Busiku

Founder & Executive Director

Brian Busiku was born and raised in Kampala, Uganda. From a young age, he was deeply affected by the number of children living on the streets in his community. After completing his degree in Social Work at Makerere University, he dedicated his life to helping these vulnerable children.

Brian started Courage Charity with a simple mission: to provide street children with the basic necessities of life and the opportunity to build a better future. His hands-on approach and deep understanding of the challenges faced by street children have been instrumental in the organization's success.

Under Brian's leadership, Courage Charity has grown from a small grassroots initiative to an organization that has transformed hundreds of lives. He continues to oversee all programs and maintains direct contact with the children we serve.

Every child deserves a safe home, an education, and the chance to dream. At Courage Charity, we believe that with the right support, every street child can overcome their circumstances and build a bright future.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Compassion

We approach every child with empathy, understanding, and unconditional love, recognizing their inherent worth and dignity.

Protection

We prioritize the safety and well-being of every child in our care, creating environments where they can heal and thrive.

Education

We believe education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and empowering children to create better futures.

Collaboration

We work closely with local communities, government agencies, and other organizations to maximize our impact.

Community

We believe in the power of community to support and uplift its most vulnerable members, particularly children.

Sustainability

We focus on long-term solutions that empower children and families to become self-sufficient and resilient.
