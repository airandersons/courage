About Courage Charity

Learn about our mission, vision, and founder

Our Mission

Courage Charity Organization is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and reintegrating street children in Kampala, Uganda. We provide safe shelter, quality education, healthcare, and psychological support to help these children build a better future.

Our Vision

We envision a Kampala where no child has to live on the streets, where every child has access to education, healthcare, and the opportunity to reach their full potential in a loving and supportive environment.

Brian Busiku - Founder

Brian Wambeso grew up in Kampala and witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by street children. After completing his education in social work, he founded Courage Charity in 2015 to address this critical issue. His dedication has helped transform the lives of hundreds of children.

Our Programs

Comprehensive support for street children

Education & Literacy

We provide formal education, vocational training, and literacy programs to help children gain skills for a better future.

Healthcare & Nutrition

Regular medical check-ups, treatment, and balanced meals ensure the children's physical well-being and healthy development.

Safe Shelter

We provide a safe, loving home environment where children can heal, grow, and feel secure away from the dangers of the street.
