Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I volunteer with Courage Charity?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including teaching, mentoring, administrative support, and event coordination. Please fill out our volunteer application form (available on our website) or contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteers@couragecharity.org. All volunteers undergo a background check and orientation session.

How are donations used?

85% of all donations go directly to programs helping street children, including shelter, food, education, and healthcare. 10% covers administrative costs, and 5% supports fundraising efforts. We publish annual financial reports that are available upon request.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes, we offer a child sponsorship program. Sponsors receive regular updates about their sponsored child, including photos, letters, and progress reports. The monthly sponsorship fee of $50 covers education, healthcare, nutrition, and other essentials. Contact us for more information about available children for sponsorship.

How can my company partner with Courage Charity?

We welcome corporate partnerships through employee volunteering programs, matching gift programs, cause-related marketing, and direct sponsorship of programs. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@couragecharity.org to discuss opportunities.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, Courage Charity is a registered nonprofit organization in Uganda (Registration No. 12345/2015). Donations from Ugandan residents are tax-deductible. For international donors, we provide donation receipts that may be tax-deductible in your country. Please consult with your tax advisor for specific information.

How can I organize a fundraiser for Courage Charity?

We welcome community fundraisers! We provide resources including logos, photos, and information packets to help you organize successful events. Contact our fundraising team at fundraising@couragecharity.org for support and to discuss your ideas.
